About

Sixteen years of turning messy operational data into decisions.

I'm a data analyst and BI developer who has spent the last 16+ years inside Healthcare, Health Insurance, and Life Sciences organizations — building the pipelines, models, and dashboards that let payer, provider, and pharma teams make decisions on numbers they can defend.

My work spans the full path from source system to boardroom slide: designing ETL and data warehouse layers, modeling claims and provider data, and leading several Tableau-to-Power BI migrations that preserved KPI accuracy while improving performance and governance.
